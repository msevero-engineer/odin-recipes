Easy Guacamole

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

This quick and easy guacamole is (almost) too good to be true.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
